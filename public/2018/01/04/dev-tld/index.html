Chrome 63: The End of .dev for Local Environments

January 4, 2018

After returning to work from the holiday break, I began to work on a local Magento 1 site that had the TLD .dev. I quickly discovered something was wrong. After struggling for about an hour to figure out why Chrome was giving me issues and Safari was not, I learned that since the release of Chrome 63, .dev is now redirected to HTTPS via HSTS (HTTP Strict Transport Security) and that was the cause of my problems. In this post I will quickly go over how to update your local Magento 1 site using MAMP.
